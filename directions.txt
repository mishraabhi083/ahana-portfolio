# AI Portfolio Creation Direction Map

## Personal Brand: Ahana Chowdhury

This document is a strategic blueprint for an AI design/development agent to create a high-end, modern, conversion-focused portfolio website that positions Ahana as a premium Digital Marketing, Growth, and Brand Strategy professional.

The portfolio should feel like a mix of:

* Growth marketer
* Startup operator
* Brand strategist
* Performance marketer
* Community builder

The overall experience should communicate:

> “A growth-focused marketer who combines creativity, analytics, branding, SEO, paid marketing, and community building to scale businesses.”

The portfolio must achieve these goals:

### Primary Goals

* Attract recruiters for:

  * Performance Marketing
  * Social Media Strategy
  * Influencer Marketing
* Position Ahana as:

  * Startup-growth capable

* Generate freelance/consulting opportunities
* Showcase measurable business impact
* Demonstrate marketing versatility
* Build authority and personal branding

# 3. Design Language

## UI Style

Use:

* Glassmorphism subtly
* Soft shadows
* Large typography
* Clean whitespace
* Interactive cards
* Animated metrics
* Scroll storytelling
* Dark/light adaptive design

## Typography

Use modern font pairing:

* Headings → Bold geometric sans-serif
* Body → Clean readable sans-serif

Recommended:

* Inter
* Satoshi
* Space Grotesk
* Manrope

## Color Palette

Primary vibe:

* Black / White base
* Accent gradients
* Electric purple / blue / pink
  OR
* Elegant warm tones with premium gold accents

Use vibrant gradients for:

* KPI cards
* CTA sections
* Analytics visuals
* Social growth sections
